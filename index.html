Welcome to Team 4's JOUR479K project!

Objective: Our objective for this project was to make a site that makes it easy for DJ's on college campuses to receive alerts when new and popular music comes out that would interest their audience.

Get to know our site: Click around and look for your favorite bands. We get all of our information from the most reliable music news sources on the web. We're big fans of Rolling Stone, Pitch Fork and AV Club. All of our sources should make it easy for music lovers to trust our website and what we are syndicating.

Authors and Contributors

Phoebe Dinner, Carols Rodriguez and Gabe Fernandez came up with this idea to rock the music world.
